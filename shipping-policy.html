Shipping Policy

Processing & Dispatch

We aim to process and dispatch all orders promptly once payment has cleared. Orders are typically packed and shipped within 1–3 working days, excluding weekends and UK bank holidays.

Delivery Times

Delivery times vary depending on the service selected at checkout and your location. All delivery times are estimates only and are not guaranteed. Shipping costs are shown at checkout before you place your order.

Dispatch Confirmation

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email. Where available, tracking details will be included so you can follow your delivery.

Delivery Address

Please ensure your delivery address is accurate and complete when placing your order. We cannot be responsible for delays or non-delivery caused by incorrect or incomplete address details.

Delayed, Damaged, or Missing Orders

If your order is delayed, arrives damaged, or appears to be missing, please contact us as soon as possible at sales@littlegreenacorn.com. We will investigate with the carrier and work with you to resolve the issue in line with your statutory rights.
